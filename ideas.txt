Tilesets: Different room ideas unique to each dungeon that allows for more engaging gameplay.
    -Earth
        - Shrines: Create a series of statues that randomly spawn throughout the dungeon. These statues will have various positive and negative effects
        - Boulder Room: Have boulder objects spawn and randomly move around the room. Boulders deal high damage
        - Quicksand Room: Generate patches of quicksand that slowly swallow player, causing death if the player stays on them for too long.
        - Golem Room: Mini-boss room. Golem is big and slow with multiple attacks depending on distance from player. Can either throw a small boulder or slam the ground around them.
        - Stalagmite Room: Create terrain patches with spikes that damage the player when the player moves over them. 
        - 
    -Water
        - 
        - River Room: Have a water object that spans across the room in a random pattern. Causes player to move along the river in a set direction.
        - Waterfall room: Big waterfall with a pool at its bottom spawns along one of the walls. Water spirit spawns in the pool with a chance for a chest to be spawned within/behind the waterfall. 
        - Water Spirit Room: Water pools in this room contain water spirits that are invulnerable but stationary enemies. They shoot water projectiles at the player.
    -Ice
        - 
        -Freeze Room: Ice crystal objects periodically emits a freezing aura which temporarily slows the player if they enter the aura.
        -Ice Spirit Room: Ice spirits (chain of three small connected rectangles) swarm the player. They fly towards the player to attack them, then move away from the player before moving to attack again.

Runes: Collectible items that persist until a dungeon is cleared. Runes grant progressively powerful bonuses with unique tradeoffs
    Here's the full expanded rune system across all three tiers:

---

## Stat runes — 12 total

Simple numerical trade-offs. Common drops, easy to evaluate at a glance.

**Offensive (4)**
- **Bloodthirst** — +50% damage on your first hit in a room / all subsequent hits in that room deal −20% damage
- **Glass Cannon** — +80% damage / −50% max HP
- **Executioner** — +100% damage against enemies below 25% HP / −30% damage against enemies above 25% HP
- **Berserker** — +5% damage stacking per kill in a room / resets to 0 on taking any damage

**Defensive (4)**
- **Ironhide** — +60 max HP / −20% movement speed
- **Turtle Shell** — +40% damage reduction / cannot dodge
- **Last Stand** — +200% damage reduction when below 15% HP / no damage reduction above 15% HP
- **Thorns** — reflect 30% of damage taken back to attacker / your own attacks deal −25% damage

**Mobility (4)**
- **Sprinter** — +50% movement speed / −30% attack speed
- **Slippery** — +40% dodge distance / dodge has a 2-second cooldown instead of 0.5 seconds
- **Ghost Step** — pass through enemies while dodging / dodging no longer grants invincibility frames
- **Momentum** — +10% damage stacking per second of continuous movement / dealing damage resets the stack

---

## Behavior runes — 6 total

Change how abilities function rather than tuning their numbers. Uncommon drops.

**Attack behavior (3)**
- **Ricochet** — your attacks bounce to a second nearby enemy / cannot hit the same enemy twice per attack
- **Shockwave** — attacks send a slow-moving shockwave forward that deals 150% damage / your direct attack hitbox is removed entirely, you only deal damage through shockwaves
- **Vampiric Strike** — kills heal you for 8 HP / you can no longer pick up health potions or use healing shrines

**Ability behavior (3)**
- **Afterimage** — dodging leaves a damaging decoy at your origin point that lasts 2 seconds / dodge distance is halved
- **Overclock** — your active ability fires twice in rapid succession / active ability cooldown is tripled
- **Chain Reaction** — applying any status effect to an enemy spreads it to all enemies within 3 tiles / you are also affected by any status effect you apply

---

## Identity runes — 3 total

Fundamentally rewrite the character's game plan. Legendary drops, maximum one per run.

- **The Pacifist** — you deal zero direct damage; enemies who collide with each other take 500% of your normal damage; your movement speed is doubled; picking up any weapon item destroys this rune permanently
- **Glass Soul** — your max HP is locked at 1 permanently; every source of damage instead triggers a 2-second invincibility window; healing items convert into temporary attack speed boosts; being stunned kills you instantly
- **Time Anchor** — time slows to 20% speed while you stand completely still; moving instantly snaps time back to normal speed; a hidden patience meter fills while slowed and empties while moving — releasing a fully charged meter triggers a 3-second time freeze for all enemies
